Apple is a fruit that grows on trees.
It comes in various colors like red, green, and yellow.
Apples are rich in fiber and vitamins.
They make a healthy and convenient snack.
Apples can be eaten raw or cooked in various dishes.
They are commonly used in pies, cakes, and other desserts.
Some people also make apple juice and apple cider.
Apple trees typically bloom in spring with beautiful white or pink flowers.
